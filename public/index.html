⚽ The Boyz Pool — Rules

  • Entry fee: $10 per person
  • Each player picks 8 teams from the 48 in the tournament
  • Teams are first-come, first-served — once picked, no one else can take it
  • Teams can be swapped between players before the tournament starts
  • Once the tournament begins, all picks are final
  • The player with the most total points wins — points add up across all 8 of your teams
  • Tiebreaker: whoever has a team that went furthest in the tournament wins
